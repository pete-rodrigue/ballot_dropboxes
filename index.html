Where can I drop off my ballot?
Search the map to find a ballot dropbox in your county!

Remember that you have to drop off your ballot by 8pm on November 3rd!

If you need to check your voter registration, you can do that here: vote.utah.gov

If you're unsure about a location, you can contact your county election office:

Utah County Elections: 801-851-8128 (website)

Sanpete County Elections: 435-835-2131 Ext. 5 (website)

Salt Lake County Elections: 385-468-7400 (website)

Juab County Elections: 435-623-3410 (website)



Note: this map doesn't necessarily show all ballot dropbox locations! If any of these locations seem wrong, please don't hesitate to call or text (385) 831-1143. We'll do our best to fix things right away.
