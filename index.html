Where can I drop off my ballot?
Search the map to find a ballot dropbox in your county!

Remember that you have to drop off your ballot by 8pm on November 3rd!

If you're unsure about a location, you can call your county election office at these numbers:

Utah County Elections: 801-851-8128

Sanpete County Elections: 435-835-2131 Ext. 5

Salt Lake County Elections: 385-468-7400

Juab County Elections: 435-623-3410
